About Inventory Xpress

Inventory Xpress is a versatile and user-friendly project designed for effortless product management. This system allows users to easily add, edit, and delete product records across various categories.

With a clean and intuitive interface, users can input crucial details such as product name, description, category, price, and quantity. The project incorporates dynamic CRUD operations, empowering users to Create, Read, Update, and Delete records as needed.

Add Product

The "Add Product" feature allows you to easily add new products to your inventory. Clicking this button opens an intuitive form where you can input product details.

Delete

The "Delete" feature empowers you to remove unwanted products from your inventory. Easily delete records with a simple click, ensuring efficient management.

Update Product

The "Update Product" feature facilitates seamless modification of product details. Edit existing records effortlessly by clicking the update button and making necessary changes.
